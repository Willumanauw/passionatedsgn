A little about me. 

As designers, our area of expertise and focus is inadvertently ALWAYS visual design. We begin our processes at research, establish design principles and style guides, and end up creating pixel perfect visual designs. Through several rounds and various types of testing, we determine if these designs can be deemed fit for our users to consume.
